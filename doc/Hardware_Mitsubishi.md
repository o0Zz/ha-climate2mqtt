# Introduction

This document provides details about the Mitsubishi heat pump **image connector** and its corresponding pinout.

## Image Connector

![Mitsubishi Connector](mitsubishi_connector.jpg)

### Connector Pinout

| **Pin** | **Signal Description**      | **Wire Color** | **Notes** |
|:--------:|-----------------------------|----------------|-----------|
| 1 | 12V *(Not used)* | N/A | — |
| 2 | GND | Yellow | Ground connection |
| 3 | 5V | White | Power supply |
| 4 | TX — Data from heat pump | Black | Transmit line |
| 5 | RX — Data from thermostat | Red | Receive line |
